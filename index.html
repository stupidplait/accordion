FAQ

I have an unusual apartment. Are your solutions suitable for unique layouts?

We will make a project for any unique layout and take into account the features of your apartment.

I have suggestions for changing the design, can you take this into account?

If you have chosen a ready-made design from our catalog and want to make changes to colors or types of materials, just let us know - we will take this into account in the drawings.

The interior images show a studio living room, and I have two separate rooms, is this exactly right for me?

We adapt the chosen design to any area and configuration of the room, taking into account all the features.

I liked the design of the bathroom with a shower, but we will definitely have a bathtub in our apartment. Can it be replaced?

Yes, of course, we use the same combinations of materials, colors and textures, but we use a bath or shower at your request.

Where can I find materials from your interiors?

For each room we provide a list of materials used, names of paints, descriptions and names of furniture. And also a separate sheet with a list of suppliers from whom you can buy everything.

Why do we need drawings?

Without drawings, builders will do the work at their discretion and most likely you will have to constantly correct them. It is unlikely that you want to specify a place for each of the 40 outlets manually. For you, drawings are a tool for monitoring repairs, and for builders - a guarantee that they do everything the way you need.
